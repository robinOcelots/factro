Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Fab Invest ("we", "us", or "our"). We are committed to protecting your personal contact information and your right to privacy. If you have any questions about our policy and practices, please contact us at support@fractro.in.

At Fab Invest, we operate the www.fabinvest.in website, which provides financial and investment opportunities and consulting services. This policy explains the information we collect, how we use it, and your rights. Please review it thoroughly.

This policy applies to all information collected through our website and related services, sales, marketing, or events ("Services").

2. What Information Do We Collect?

Information You Provide

We collect personal information you provide when interacting with our Services. This may include:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Other personal details during service use
Information We Collect Automatically

We may automatically collect device info, IP address, browser type, and behavior using cookies and tracking tools to improve our services.

3. How Do We Use Your Information?

  • Providing Services

    To process requests, manage your account, respond to inquiries, and personalize your experience.

  • Marketing and Communication

    With consent, we may send promotions and updates. You can opt out at any time.

  • Legal and Compliance

    We use your information to meet legal obligations and prevent fraud.

4. Will Your Information Be Shared With Anyone?

  • Third-Party Service Providers

    We may share information with trusted providers (e.g., payment processors, KYC agencies) who are contractually obligated to protect it.

  • Business Transfers

    In case of mergers or acquisitions, your data may be transferred, but protection commitments remain.

  • Legal Requirements

    We may disclose data to comply with laws, legal processes, or protect rights and safety.

5. Security Measures

We implement encryption, secure servers, and access control, but no method is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Redirection to Third-Party Websites

Some Services may link to external websites (e.g., for payments). These have their own privacy policies, which you should review.

7. Data Retention

We retain data as long as necessary for service delivery, legal, or operational reasons, and delete it securely once no longer needed.

8. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access, Update or Delete Your Information

    Contact us at support@fractro.in to manage your data.

  • Opting Out of Communication

    You may unsubscribe via email or contact us to stop all communications.

9. Children's Privacy

We don't knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe this occurred, contact us to remove it.

10. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Check this page for updates. For concerns, email support@fractro.in.
