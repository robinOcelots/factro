FAQs

Frequently asked questions

What is Fab Invest?

Fab Invest is a Real Estate Investment platform that facilitates small and mid-scale investors to micro invest in premier real estate projects and earn upto 18% Returns per annum from these Investments. Your Investment in the Project will be protected by a property surety worth at least 1.5 times your investment value.

What is Base Investor Price?

Investors in real estate projects acquire shares at a deeply discounted rate (40-50% off market price). This is known as Base Investor Price and usually reflects the builder's cost without profit or overhead.

What is the minimum investment to start with?

You can start investing on our platform with a minimum investment of ₹25 Lakhs.

What is the investment term?

Terms range from 12 to 36 months. Each opportunity has specific durations listed on its respective page.

How does the repayment work?

Repayments are scheduled per deal terms. Profits are paid every 6–12 months; principal is repaid at term end.

How does Fab Invest curate Investment Opportunities?

With 15+ years of experience, we follow strict scrutiny. Less than 5% of projects reviewed pass our filters.

What are your Investment Principles?

We only invest in RERA-approved, late-stage projects worth ₹100+ crores, offering 1.5x+ entry points.

Fractionalizing Real Estate Investments!!

Instead of 1 investor with ₹5 Cr+, Fab Invest pools multiple investors to access builder-backed investments with 1.7x–2x surety.

What is Fractional Ownership?

Multiple people own shares in a large property via a company. Each investor owns equity in the company holding the property.

Is Investing riskier than Buying a Flat?

Investing is 2x safer due to discounted share price and legal ownership via registration, just like buying a property.

How is the Investment process executed?

Each investment is via a dedicated LLP, and collateral is registered in the LLP’s name. Investors hold proportional shares.

Can I withdraw my money before the term ends?

Early exits take 3-4 months minimum. Not advised for emergency funds. Returns reduce to 15% annually if exited early.

How are multiple premature withdrawal requests processed?

Requests are honored on a first-come-first-serve basis using liquidity from collateral sales.

What happens if the Developer defaults the payment?

Fab Invest will liquidate the 1.5x–2x secured asset and distribute proceeds as per shareholding.

What is the risk? Should I invest with Fab Invest?

While there's risk, even in the worst case, you acquire property at ~40% below market. Know your risk appetite before investing.

Does Fab Invest guarantee my money?

No guarantees. We’re a facilitator. Do your due diligence and consult financial advisors before investing.

What if Fab Invest shuts down its operations?

All agreements are between investors and developers. Assets and contracts remain enforceable regardless of our platform status.
